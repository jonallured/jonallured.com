Attitudes about Development

I believe what makes someone really good at designing and developing software is having empathy for users. You have to care about what its like to be a hesitant web user and take their needs seriously. This is the attitude I bring to my work and its what I'm most proud of.

Bringing clarity to projects is as much about what you leave out as what you include. Sure, its about great copy and usable interfaces, but its also about saying no. I care about keeping the things I work on focused on what's really important.

Being a part of the technology industry means being open to constantly learning new things. Success means more than that though—you need to be internally motivated to do this. You have to want to learn, to see it as your responsibility to teach yourself things. That's how I learned Rails and its what I love about working in this field.
